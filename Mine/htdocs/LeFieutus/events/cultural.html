Foot Loose

There are shortcuts to happiness and dancing is one of them.

Participate and surrender yourself to the sound of music in the western Dance competition “footloose” to dazzle everybody with your moves.

Rules:

• Time allotted to each group is restricted to a maximum of 6 minutes.
• A group can have minimum 8 and maximum 14 members.
• At any point of time, there should not be less than 2 members performing on the stage.
• Participants need to carry their own props if any.
• Participants need to make sure that any kind of stunt being included in the act is be safe.
• There will be a preliminary round in which the groups need to show a glimpse of their final act in a time limit of 3-4 minutes.
• Participants need to carry their tracks in a pen drive.
• Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
• The decision of the judges shall be final.
• Two copies of soundtrack are to be submitted standard audio format- .wav or.mp3 of sound quality 192kbps or above to the organizers (in C.Ds only, no laptops).
• All groups need to have the approval of their respective colleges/universities.

Time : Day 1
Venue : Main Stage

Vogue

"Clothed with strength & dignity, glamour & grace, they all smiled for the days to come ".

Participate in fashion parade and enhance the glamour of the night.
Rules:
• There should be 10-18 participants per team exclusive of the designer, choreographer and supporting members.

• Teams have to arrange their own costumes and props.
• Use of materials like cigarettes, alcohol and any hazardous materials on stage is prohibited .Teams are required to contact organizers before using such stuff.
• Two copies of soundtrack are to be submitted standard audio format- .wav or.mp3 of sound quality 192kbps or above to the organizers(in C.Ds only,no laptops).
• Vulgarity of any kind would lead to disqualification of the team from the event. Hence,if the team feels that any stunt of costume design can be deemed as vulgar,it is strongly advised to consult organizers before performing.

• It is imperative that all team members possess a valid college id which must be presented at the time of registration.

Tumse Na Ho Paega

It will feature a number of exciting one-minute fun games which will set up difficult challenges for players. Fun is what all there will be.
• Cookie face: Test your control of your facial muscles with this simple fun game by moving one to three cookies from your forehead to your mouth without using your hands.
• Gun Shooting: A game that does pretty much what it says on the tin, you shoot balloons. This vibrant, colorful game will require lightning fast reflexes and a remarkably steady hand.
• Button Biryani: An interesting one-minute game to be played with rice and buttons in which players have to pick the buttons from rice bowl.
• Pick if you can: A game in which players have to pick the fruits(grapes) floating in a water bowl with a toothpick.
And many more…..
