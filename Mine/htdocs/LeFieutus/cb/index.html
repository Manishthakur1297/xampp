About

Nakash Aziz is an Indian playback singer and music composer. He has assisted the legendary composer A. R. Rahman on films like Highway, Raanjhanaa,Rockstar, Delhi 6 and I in Hindi. He is popularly known for playback of songs like "Sari Ke Fall Sa" and "Gandi Baat" from the film R... Rajkumar (2013) and "Dhating Nach" from film Phata Poster Nikhla Hero (2013); all picturized on Shahid Kapoor. Nakash is originally from Moodbidri, a small town near Mangalore, Karnataka. He belongs to a family of singers. He worked as music composer for TV serials before becoming a playback singer. JYC presenting him for the first time in JUIT .
